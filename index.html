About Us

Malaysia Job Market Insights is a platform that offers a clear, visual understanding of the current employment landscape in Malaysia. By analyzing job data from leading portals like JobStreet, Indeed, and LinkedIn, we provides insights into hiring trends, top industries, and market performance.

Our goal is to help job seekers, employers, and policy makers make informed decisions based on reliable data — not guesswork. Through our interactive dashboard, users can explore employment patterns and opportunities across Malaysia’s job market.

We aim to empower Malaysians with accurate data to support better career planning, recruitment strategies, and workforce development.


About the Dashboard

The interactive dashboard is built using Microsoft Power BI and is structured into four core sections:

  • Overview: A summary of unemployment rate in Malaysia for the previous years.
  • Trend: Visualizations that show job demand and salary analysis across different sectors and location.
  • Analysis: Deep dives into the job market performance by measuring the Gross Domestic Product performance.
  • Prediction: Forecasting models that predict future job market using past trends and insights.

Each section is designed to help users explore the Malaysian job market in an intuitive and data-rich format.
